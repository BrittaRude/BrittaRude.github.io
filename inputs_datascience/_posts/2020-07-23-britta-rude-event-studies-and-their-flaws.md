## Event Studies - Giving them a critical close-up

As already mentioned in my previous blog post about [what event studies are](https://brittarude.github.io/blog/2020/07/22/britta-rude-event-study) a recent paper by [Roth (2020)](https://scholar.harvard.edu/files/jroth/files/roth_pretrends_testing.pdf) gives event studies a critical close-up, especially about the fact that they are often used to test parallel trend assumptions.
